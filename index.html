Steam Rules (Summary)
🔒
30-day Release Cooldown — no discounts for 30 days post-launch. Seasonal sales are NOT exempt from this.
⏳
30-day Discount Cooldown — 30 days between discounts. Seasonal sales ARE exempt — you can enter or exit a seasonal sale regardless of this cooldown.
🌤️
Seasonal exemption is two-way — you can discount within 30 days before OR after a seasonal sale.
🏷️
Launch Discount — exempt from seasonal discount cooldowns (same as standard discounts).
